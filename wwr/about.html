Our History

Founded in 2005, we have been offering unforgettable rafting trips for over 20 years, creating lasting memories for adventurers of all skill levels. With a deep passion for the outdoors, we began with a small team of dedicated guides and a shared vision to provide thrilling and safe rafting experiences. Over the years, our commitment to excellence and environmental responsibility has earned us the trust of countless guests. Today, we continue to grow, expanding our reach while staying true to our roots of adventure, safety, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide the best rafting experiences that not only thrill and excite but also prioritize the safety and well-being of our guests. We are committed to ensuring that every adventure is unforgettable, with a strong emphasis on environmental stewardship. Our goal is to protect and preserve the natural beauty of the waterways we explore, while offering expert guidance and fostering a deep respect for nature.
